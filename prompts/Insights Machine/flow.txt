Sales Data Insights Generator (SDIG): Generates insights and customer profiles from the given sales data.
Sales Rep Insights Generator (SRIG): Processes insights from the Sales Data Insights Generator and combines them with the sales reps' experiences, preferences, and situational knowledge to generate potential opportunities and cautions.
Research Agent (RA): Collects all insights generated by the first two agents (SDIG and SRIG), identifies important points, generates follow-up questions for key insights, and combines insights for additional findings.
Execution Agent (EA): Asks follow-up questions to the SDIG and SRIG agents, awaits their responses, combines the information, and generates a comprehensive report outlining recommended actions for the Sales Manager.
